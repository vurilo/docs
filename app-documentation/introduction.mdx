### About Vurilo TMIS
Vurilo TMIS is a comprehensive learning management solution designed specifically for your closed group. It provides a centralized platform for delivering, 
managing, and tracking training programs tailored to the unique needs and goals of your organization.A corporate trainer seeking to streamline employee onboarding, 
or an organization aiming to scale your training initiatives, Vurilo TMIS provides the tools and resources you need to succeed.
With Vurilo TMIS, we aim to streamline learning processes, enhance knowledge retention, and foster continuous growth and development.
### Purpose of the App Help Guide
This guide serves as your go-to resource for understanding and maximizing the features and functionalities of the Vurilo TMIS application. Whether you're new to the app or seeking to deepen your understanding of its capabilities, this guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and troubleshooting solutions to ensure a smooth and productive user experience.
