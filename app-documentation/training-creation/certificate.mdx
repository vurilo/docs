**Certificate Generation Setup:**

Ensure participants receive recognition for completing training programs by setting up certificate generation in Academy Kit. Follow these steps to configure the certificate generation process within the training section:

1. **Access Training Settings:**
   - Log in to your Academy Kit account with administrative privileges.
   - Navigate to the training management section from the main menu.

2. **Select Training Program:**
   - Choose the specific training program for which you want to set up certificate generation.

3. **Access Certificate Settings:**
   - Within the training settings, locate the certificate option.

4. **Configure Certificate Fields:**
   - Specify which dynamic fields should be populated with relevant information from the training program.
     - Participant's Name (Dynamic)
     - Training Title
     - Start Date
     - End Date
     - Trainer or Administrator's Name and Designation
     - Trainer or Administrator's Signature

5. **Upload Signature:**
   - Upload the digital signature of the authorized person (e.g., trainer, administrator) who will sign the certificates. Ensure the signature file is in the appropriate format (e.g., PNG, JPEG) and meets any size or resolution requirements.

6. **Preview Certificate Generation:**
   - Before issuing certificate for participants, it's recommended to preview the generated certificates to ensure accuracy and completeness.

By following these steps, you can seamlessly integrate certificate generation into your training programs in Academy Kit, providing participants with tangible recognition of their accomplishments and motivating them to engage actively in the learning process.
